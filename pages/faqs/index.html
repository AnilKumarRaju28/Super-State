A. Product Basics

What is Sleep Well and how does it work? +
Sleep Well is a daily, science-backed dark chocolate ritual designed to help you relax, fall asleep faster, and wake up refreshed. It works by providing clinical doses of ingredients like Magnesium L-Threonate and L-Theanine to calm the nervous system without acting as a sedative.
Is this a sedative or sleep medication? +
No. This is not a sedative. It is formulated to support your body's natural sleep-wake cycle naturally.
What makes SuperState different? +
Radical transparency. We don't use proprietary blends. Every ingredient is clearly listed with its clinical dosage and scientific rationale.
Who is this product for? +
Anyone who struggles to unwind at night, experiences racing thoughts before bed, or wakes up feeling groggy and tired.
What does it taste like? +
It tastes like a rich, premium dark chocolate drink. It’s naturally sweetened and acts as a soothing evening treat.

C. Usage & Results

When should I take it and how? +
Mix one sachet into warm milk or water 30–60 minutes before you intend to sleep.

You may feel calmer on night one. Optimal results build over 7–14 days of consistent daily use.
Is it safe to take every night long-term? +
Yes. There are no dependencies formed, and the ingredients are safe for daily use.
